Hello! I'm David Vonk.

I am a student at Zuyd University of Applied Sciences, where I study Communication & Multimedia Design. Here, I learn everything about designing user-friendly and appealing digital interfaces and communication concepts. I have gained experience in graphic design, web development, videography and photography, I enjoy combining creative and technical skills. Take a look at my work to see how I apply my knowledge across various projects and what I have achieved so far.

cool stuff I made

Here are some of the projects I've worked on, combining creativity and technical skills. Each project reflects my passion for design, development, and user experience. Check them out to see how I bring ideas to life!

The Design Papers

For this project, I chose to feature Kanye West as a fashion designer because of his revolutionary and influential approach to streetwear. His unique style has pushed the boundaries of traditional fashion and made a significant impact on the modern fashion industry.

Reading Campaign

I developed **“Fiction Fans”**, a reading campaign for young people to motivate and support their reading by encouraging them to create original stories with their favorite characters—turning reading into a creative, personal experience.

Generative-Art

I developed a generative art installation inspired by the yin-yang concept. Leveraging Arduino technology, the piece reacted in real time to musical input, translating sound frequencies into evolving visual patterns that highlight the interplay of balance and contrast.

Doodle Code

For this project, we developed workshops to teach children how to code. We organized sessions where kids could explore programming languages in a fun, interactive way, supported by an engaging brand identity designed to spark their interest and keep them motivated.
