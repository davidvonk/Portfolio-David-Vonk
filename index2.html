the design papers

The Design Papers is a project where I explored the influence of Kanye West on the fashion industry. For this project, I was free to choose my own subject, and I decided to focus on Kanye because of the major impact he has had on modern fashion and sneaker culture. I looked into how his ideas and designs changed the way people see streetwear and luxury fashion. Through his Yeezy brand, Kanye introduced a new style that became very popular worldwide. His designs are known for their minimalistic look, unique shapes, and neutral colour palettes, which have inspired many other brands and designers. On this page, I present a photo gallery featuring self-made photographs of Yeezy sneakers. These photos were created to highlight the design details, textures, and overall aesthetic of the shoes. The gallery shows my interest in both fashion photography and product design, while also connecting to the research behind this project. With The Design Papers, I combined research, photography, and visual presentation to explore how one designer can have a strong influence on an entire industry.
