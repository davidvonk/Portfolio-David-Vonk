the design papers

For this project, I had to choose a specific topic to focus on. I chose Kanye West and his influence on the fashion industry. As part of the assignment, I was required to design a website that included a page or blog dedicated to this subject. In addition to the website, I also had to create original photographs that visually connected to the theme and supported the overall story of Kanye’s impact on fashion.
