fiction fans

For this project, I had to create and design a reading campaign targeting children who are unsure or hesitant about reading. During my research, I explored different communities within the reading world and discovered the world of fan fiction. This became the main inspiration for my concept: "Fiction Fans." The idea was to develop a website where users could create their own personalized stories using characters from various pop culture universes. Instead of writing everything themselves, the stories could be generated automatically based on the user’s choices. This lowered the barrier to entry and made storytelling more accessible and fun for the target group. Alongside the website concept, I also designed promotional posters to support and visually communicate the campaign.
