Generative-Art

For this project, I had to choose a topic that I felt personally connected to. I chose Yin and Yang, as balance is something I value deeply in both my personal life and creative journey. The concept of harmony between opposites felt like a meaningful foundation for my work. As part of the assignment, I created a piece of generative art using an Arduino, which was coded in Processing. The artwork was designed to be interactive, responding in real time to the music being played. The visual elements shifted dynamically with the audio, symbolizing the continuous movement and balance between contrasting forces — much like Yin and Yang itself. In addition to the generative art, I also produced a video that explained the philosophy of Yin and Yang and why it holds such personal significance for me. The video provided a more emotional and reflective layer to the project, helping to communicate the deeper meaning behind the visuals and the concept.
