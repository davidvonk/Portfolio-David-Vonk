doodle code

For this project, we were tasked with designing a workshop for high school students centered around coding. The focus was specifically on creating simple drawings using p5.js, a JavaScript-based creative coding library that runs in the browser. Our goal was to make coding feel playful, accessible, and creative, especially for students who may not have prior experience. That’s how the concept "Doodle Code" was born: a fun and approachable brand aimed at introducing young people to the basics of coding through visual expression. I also developed a full brand identity around the Doodle Code concept, including a logo, color palette, and visual style that reflected the playful, educational, and hands-on nature of the workshop. The identity was designed to feel inviting and inspiring to the target audience, while still being functional and adaptable for future use in both print and digital media.
