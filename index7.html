Who Am I?

People often describe me as easy-going and approachable. While I sometimes hesitate to step out of my comfort zone, I’m always open to new experiences and growth. I love creating beautiful and functional designs and capturing moments that tell a story.

What Are My Skills?

Throughout my studies, I have explored a wide range of disciplines such as programming, film, photography, design, and video editing. Over time, I discovered that my interests mostly lie in graphic design, videography, and photography. My skills in these areas are at a developing level, and I’m committed to further honing them. I have experience with Adobe software, figma, and editing techniques, and I enjoy turning creative ideas into visual concepts.

What Drives Me?

As I move into the later years of my studies, I am focusing on the creative fields that excite me the most. My goal is to sharpen my design, video, and photography skills and to gain as much hands-on experience as possible.
